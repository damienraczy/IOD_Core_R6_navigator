# Strategic Maturity Levels and Learning Loops (S6)

|LL|Level|Description|
|---|---|---|
|0|Incoherent|Decisions are purely reactive, random, or contradictory; no coherent value proposition or strategic direction exists.|
|1|Implicit|Strategy exists in culture or history but remains unexamined; the organisation repeats past patterns and adapts only through forced improvisation when crisis strikes.|
|2|Declared|Strategy is formally stated but disconnected from budgets and daily decisions; corrections target actions (processes) rather than underlying assumptions.|
|3|Assumed|Strategy is enacted and aligned with resource allocation; the organisation periodically questions its assumptions and pivots intentionally when needed.|
|4|Regulated|The organisation consciously alternates between stability (exploitation) and adaptation (exploration), governing the tension as a dynamic system rather than a static plan.|
