# ISO Maturity Levels

| ISO | Level       | Description                                                                                                           |
| --- | ----------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 0   | Incomplete  | Capability not identified, not structured, or ineffective; no reliable practices in place.                            |
| 1   | Initial     | Informal practices relying on individuals; outcomes depend on personal initiative rather than defined processes.      |
| 2   | Managed     | Practices are planned and monitored; partially identified and tracked within a limited scope.                         |
| 3   | Established | Practices are defined and standardised; clearly documented, shared, and applied consistently across the organisation. |
| 4   | Predictable | Practices are measured and controlled; performance indicators enable active management and quantitative oversight.    |
| 5   | Innovating  | Continuous improvement and adaptation; practices are proactively refined and optimised in response to change.         |
