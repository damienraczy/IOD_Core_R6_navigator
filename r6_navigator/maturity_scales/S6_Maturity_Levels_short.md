# S6 — Strategic Pivot Maturity Levels

Reference framework for assessing strategic R6 pivot logics.
Describes the depth of strategic positioning and learning loop integration.

## Level 1 — Unreflective
Strategic choices are implicit and unexamined.
The organization operates on inherited assumptions without questioning them.
No feedback mechanism connects outcomes to strategic premises.

## Level 2 — Single-Loop Learning
The organization adjusts actions to meet existing goals.
Problems are solved within the current strategic framework.
Underlying assumptions are not questioned.

## Level 3 — Double-Loop Learning
The organization questions the premises and values behind its goals.
Strategic pivots are recognized and deliberately managed.
Leaders actively navigate tensions between stability and change.

## Level 4 — Triple-Loop / Generative
The organization reframes its identity and learning processes.
Strategic positioning is fluid, reflexive and collectively owned.
The organization generates new paradigms rather than adapting to existing ones.
