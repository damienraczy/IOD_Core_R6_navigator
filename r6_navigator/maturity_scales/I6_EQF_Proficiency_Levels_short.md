# I6 — Individual Competency Levels (EQF-adapted)

Reference framework for assessing individual R6 competencies.
Based on the European Qualifications Framework (EQF), adapted to the R6 Individual level.

## Level 1 — Foundational
The individual demonstrates basic awareness of the competency domain.
Behaviour is rule-following and requires close supervision.
Actions are reactive and context-specific.

## Level 2 — Elementary
The individual applies the competency in simple, familiar situations.
Shows some initiative within defined parameters.
Partial understanding of the underlying rationale.

## Level 3 — Developing
The individual applies the competency in varied situations with limited support.
Begins to adapt behaviour to context.
Can explain their actions with reference to relevant principles.

## Level 4 — Proficient
The individual applies the competency autonomously in complex situations.
Integrates multiple perspectives and adjusts strategies accordingly.
Actively reflects on practice and seeks improvement.

## Level 5 — Advanced
The individual demonstrates mastery and generates new approaches.
Contributes to developing practice in their domain.
Mentors others and transfers competency effectively.

## Level 6 — Expert
The individual operates at the frontier of the competency domain.
Shapes systemic change and creates enabling conditions for others.
Demonstrates exemplary alignment between values, reasoning and action.
