# O6 — Organizational Maturity Levels

Reference framework for assessing organizational R6 capacities.
Describes the degree to which an organization has developed and institutionalized a given capacity.

## Level 1 — Initial / Ad hoc
The capacity is absent or exercised sporadically.
Outcomes depend on individual heroics rather than systemic processes.
No shared understanding or standards exist.

## Level 2 — Emerging
The capacity is recognized as important and basic practices exist.
Application is inconsistent across units or contexts.
Learning is informal and not systematically captured.

## Level 3 — Defined
The capacity is formalized in documented processes and shared practices.
Application is consistent across the organization.
Accountability roles are identified.

## Level 4 — Managed
The capacity is monitored and measured.
The organization adapts processes based on feedback and data.
Continuous improvement cycles are in place.

## Level 5 — Optimizing
The capacity drives systemic innovation and organizational renewal.
The organization serves as a reference for others.
The capacity is fully integrated into strategic intent and culture.
